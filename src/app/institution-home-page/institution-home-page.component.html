About us Our Team

We are here to redefine the term Ed-Tech. Presently Ed-Tech means getting away with infrastructure and logistical costs when it could mean much more. Advancements in data sciences have been neglected in this field altogether. Analytics can lend a big helping hand to the students in their preparation and learning process. Exactly figuring out if the lack is in understanding or practice in every topic and subject and if the preparation level is apt for the examination are some of the problems which can only be tackled by a very high teacher student ratio or data analytics. We’ve envisioned this portal to be a helping hand in assessing and helping both teachers and students in better assessment and learning to get substantially better results.
